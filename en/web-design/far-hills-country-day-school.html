Far Hills Country Day School
Strategic Plan

FHCDS asked us to design and produce a printed booklet and website to communicate their Strategic Plan. The website shown here is linked to their homesite but has a unique masthead and graphic treatment specific to the identity we created for the Strategic Plan. This identity includes the spot illustration of a hand using a GPS device which helps underscore the school's dedication to preparing and educating their students to be at the forefront of emerging technologies as well as being grounded in the fundamental values of honesty, integrity and educational excellence.
