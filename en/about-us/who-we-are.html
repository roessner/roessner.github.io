Who We Are

Roessner & Co. is a strategic design firm. We're a team of seasoned creative professionals: graphic designers, writers, webmasters, videographers and production specialists with a long track record of success producing award-winning and cost-effective Annual Reports, branding, websites, marketing materials, advertising and event graphics for print, web and video.

Since 1976, we've been designing and creating projects for large and small corporations, non-profits, schools, colleges and universities. Customer-focused, strategy-driven, creative solutions delivered on-time and on budget.

Our success is based on the way we approach our work. No matter how large the company nor how small the budget, we make it our business to listen to our clients and fully understand their needs. We then search for the most appropriate solution and develop the best product in the most efficient manner with the highest level of responsive, reliable customer service.
